Our Story

Codmint was born from a simple observation — too many talented students graduate without any real-world experience. Interviews ask for portfolios that no one ever helped them build.

We set out to fix that. We designed an internship platform that gives every applicant a structured domain, real project tasks, mentor guidance, and a verifiable certificate upon completion.

From graphic design to backend engineering, from Python scripting to Flutter app development — Codmint bridges the gap between education and industry.
